since feeling

is first


who pays any attention
to the syntax of things
will never wholly kiss you;
wholly to be a fool
while Spring is in the world
my blood approves,
and kisses are better fate than wisdom lady i swear by all the flowers. Don't cry - the best gesture of my brain is less than your eyelids' flutter which says

we are for each other;

then laugh,
leaning back in my arms


for life's not a paragraph
And death i think is no parenthesis
