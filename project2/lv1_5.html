feeling                                                                                           will never wholly kiss you;


since                 is first who pays any attention to the syntax of things



                          wholly to be a fool while Spring is in the world




my blood approves, and kisses
                                                                                                                 are better fate than wisdom
                                                                                                                 lady i swear by all the flowers.

your eyelids' flutter




which says


                          we are for each other;

                          then laugh, leaning back in my arms

for life's not a paragraph
